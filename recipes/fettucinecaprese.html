Fettucine Caprese

Ingredients

  • 1 serving fettucine
  • 1/2 T butter
  • 4 campari tomatoes
  • 2 T balsamic vinegar (white balsamic is nice)
  • T olive oil
  • generous handful fresh basil
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 servings fresh mozzarella pearls
  • fresh-cracked black pepper and salt to taste

Directions

  1. Heat castiron pan to high.
  2. Wash campari tomatoes and slice into quarters.
  3. Place tomatoes skin down in castiron and allow them to blister.
  4. Remove pan from heat and replace with a pot of water and the butter.
  5. When the water boils, add the fettucine. Boil fettucine until nearly al dente, with a little of the water still in the pot, turn off heat, but keep the pot on the burner.
  6. Add the garlic, salt and black pepper to the tomatoes, and deglaze the pan by adding the balsamic vinegar. Spatula the contents of the pan into the pot of fettucine, stirring to combine well.
  7. Chiffonade the basil and fold the basil and mozzarella pearls into the contents of the pot.
  8. Serve in your favorite size bowl.
  9. Enjoy!
